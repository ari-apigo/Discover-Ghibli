About Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli is a Japanese animation studio known for its anime feature films. Founded in Tokyo in 1985, Studio Ghibli has since released 21 feature films with the 22nd in production (as of October 2019). Six of Studio Ghibli's films are among the 10 highest-grossing anime films made in Japan.

Discover Ghibli?

Each of Studio Ghibli's films take place in fictional worlds, although many are influenced by reality, such as certain towns in Japan and certain time periods like World War II. This site organizes Studio Ghibli's worlds by film. Discover Ghibli!
